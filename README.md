# Music Handler SKSE

An SKSE plugin that allows users to have greater control over the music that plays in exploration, combat, and dungeons.

Exploration Music:
- Adjust frequency of exploration music in hours.

Combat:
- Smart: Option for comabt music to play only upon hitting, being hit or spotting the opponent before they reach you.

- Low-threat: Option to disable music for low-threat enemies. (Adjustable?)
